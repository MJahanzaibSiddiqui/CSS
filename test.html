Essay on Mobile Phone: Mobile Phone is often also called “cellular phone”. It is a device mainly used for a voice call. Presently technological advancements have made our life easy. Laptop

A laptop computer, sometimes called a notebook computer by manufacturers, is a battery- or AC-powered personal computer generally smaller than a briefcase that can easily be transported and conveniently used in temporary spaces such as on airplanes, in libraries, temporary offices, and at meetings.

computer

A computer is an electronic device that was discovered by Charles Babbage. He is known as the father of computers. There are various parts in a computer. The four major parts of a computer are mouse, keyboard, monitor and CPU.
